Our Programs

Customized Mental Health and Addiction Therapy

Respecting the Individual

At Beachside Rehab, we know that each person's mental health issues are unique and that addiction is an individualized disease and we tailor treatment programs to specifically address each clients' needs. We get to know you as a person. Before you arrive at Beachside Rehab, our clinical and medical team dedicates time to understanding your mental health or addiction situation so we can customize a program that's right for you.

We're Adaptable

Unlike a hospital, Beachside Rehab is more akin to a Mental Health Retreat. We don't expect the same program to work for every person, so we provide a variety of proven therapies in a luxury, peaceful environment where you truly feel cared for. We pair counseling and traditional therapies with a mind, body, spirit focus. That includes holistic healing services and informal quiet walks on our secluded property. Beachside Rehab is Florida's premier Mental Health Treatment Center
